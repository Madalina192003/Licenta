About BOSCH

Founded in 1886, Robert Bosch GmbH has grown into one of the world's leading engineering and technology companies. With a global presence and a commitment to innovation, BOSCH continues to shape industries across automotive, industrial technology, consumer goods, and energy sectors.

Our Customer Management System is designed specifically for BOSCH's internal plant management, providing a streamlined solution for monitoring, analyzing, and optimizing client relationships across all business units.
